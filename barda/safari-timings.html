barda wildlife sanctuary Timings

The time of entry and exit for Jungle Safari in barda wildlife sanctuary Park varies according to the season.In winter, due to short periods of daylight hours, park entry time in the morning takes place shortly after the sunrise and the time to exit of the park occurs before sunset.

Season Morning Trip evening Trip
Winter 6:45 AM to 9:45 AM 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM
Summer 6:45 AM to 9:45 AM 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM

Barda Wildlife Sanctuary is located just 15 km from Porbandar. This hilly forest helps protect the area from coastal saltwater. It was declared a sanctuary in February 1979.

Forest Minister Mulubhai Bera and Jamnagar MP Poonam Madam hosted this launch, which adds a new dimension to Gujarat’s eco-tourism and wildlife conservation efforts.

Covering nearly 192 square kilometres, the sanctuary offers visitors a chance to explore Gujarat’s wildlife in a quiet, natural setting—a great alternative to the more dynamic Sasan Gir.
